Bio

Aaron Hale is a web developer living in Philadelphia, PA. He has a background in audio production and sound design along with a passion for music. Aaron has a drive to explore and challenge himself which lead him to enter the world of web development. Through his journey he found an interest in audio/video streaming technologies and their impact on our lives.

Before he began his studies he ran a busy pet care company servicing the Philadelphia area. In growing his business he learned key communication skills and a strong framework of organization and patience. He hopes to bring his skill and experience to new and exciting projects!
